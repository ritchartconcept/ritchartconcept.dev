ABOUT

Hello! I'm Fritzner Richard, a multidisciplinary graphic designer and artist specializing in comics and 2D animation, I've been working in the fields of illustration and graphic design since 2016. With several years' experience behind me, I'm also the founder of Noukat Studios, a creative start-up launched in 2022, dedicated to the production of innovative digital content, blending polished aesthetics and powerful visual storytelling.

Passionate about art and storytelling, my work is deeply rooted in a strong cultural identity and explores themes with universal appeal. Through my creations, I constantly strive to offer captivating narratives imbued with emotion, action and depth.

Alongside my artistic projects, I regularly share my know-how on social networks through online digital art courses, accessible free of charge or via premium packages. This pedagogical commitment reflects my desire to pass on my knowledge and contribute to the emergence of a new generation of creative talent.

I'm currently working on BOHIO Generation, an ambitious comic book project with Afro-futuristic influences, blending adventure, science fiction and cultural heritage. My aim is to make it a reference work in Haitian 9th art, combining graphic innovation and immersive storytelling.

My versatility, creativity and mastery of graphic production tools make me an asset to any dynamic team focused on creating original, high-quality content.
